# Country and listing opportunities

Review date: 2026-07-22.

The 2025 Appodeal eCPM report remains useful only as directional context: platform, format, region,
season, consent, category, fill, and inventory quality can move realized revenue substantially. It
should not be treated as a forecast for QuietPDF.

- [Appodeal 2025 eCPM report](https://appodeal.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/Appodeal-The-Latest-eCPM-Report-2025.pdf)

## Recommended experiment order

1. English master: United States, United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, and Ireland. Measure store
   listing conversion and D1/D7 retention before expanding creative variants.
2. German listing cluster: Germany, Austria, and Switzerland. Share design structure, but use
   country-specific performance reporting.
3. France, Japan, South Korea, and UAE: prioritize localized experiments; Japanese
   and Arabic require purpose-built typographic QA, and Arabic requires RTL review.
4. Scale markets: India, Brazil, Spanish-speaking Latin America, and Indonesia. Test acquisition
   cost and retention alongside lower/higher eCPM effects rather than assuming margin from volume.

For every experiment, evaluate listing conversion, retention, impressions per user, fill rate,
actual country eCPM, revenue per acquired user, rating movement, and uninstall effects. A higher eCPM
does not establish higher profitability.

Upload-ready German, French, Japanese, Hindi, Russian, Spanish, European Portuguese, Brazilian
Portuguese, Italian, Indonesian, Arabic, Korean, and Urdu creative families are stored under
`localized/upload-ready/`. They share the same real-UI source compositions as the English masters,
with locale-specific Android UI captures and adaptable layouts for longer and right-to-left text.
