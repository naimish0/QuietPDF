# Creative decisions

## Selected system: Quiet Confidence

The system uses a neutral #F8FAFC field, white document surfaces, #2563EB actions, and restrained
soft-blue depth. Headline typography is large and direct; most of each screenshot is a workflow or
document outcome rather than decorative brand space.

## Pass 1 decisions

- Selected Hero A: the app workspace is dominant and three recent-file cards establish real usage.
- Selected Icon Concept 1: the page silhouette and negative-space Q remain clear at 24–48 px. The
  square is full bleed and leaves mask/shadow treatment to Google Play.
- Selected scanner split: large source and corrected result panels communicate the transformation
  immediately; crop handles and the QuietPDF control panel establish authenticity.
- Selected compression split: uses truthful `Original` and `Smaller PDF` marketing labels while the
  embedded production Compose dialog demonstrates the implemented quality selection UI.
- Selected both feature graphics: Utility leads with breadth; Privacy is a second campaign/listing
  experiment focused on the real local-processing promise.

## Pass 2 refinements

Narrow reorder cards were redrawn with text-safe thumbnails, the merge output stack received concise
page titles, and marketing visuals were enlarged to reduce dead space. Reconstructed UI panels were
then replaced throughout the final phone, tablet, feature-graphic, and marketing families with real
production Compose captures from the deterministic Pixel 7a androidTest harness. All outputs were
regenerated, decoded, inventoried, and checked through contact sheets and original-resolution review.
