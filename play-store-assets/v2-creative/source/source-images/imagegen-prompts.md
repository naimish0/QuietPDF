# Supporting image generation prompts

Mode: built-in image generation. These inputs are supporting inserts only; all QuietPDF UI,
typography, icons, values, and layouts are deterministic.

## Scanner receipt source

Create a realistic top-down smartphone photograph of one fictional paper receipt lying crooked on a
pale warm-gray desk, with incorrect perspective, uneven natural window lighting, visible background,
and a mild soft shadow. The paper contains only abstract gray linework and geometric blocks—no
legible text, logos, brands, personal information, QR codes, barcodes, UI, watermark, or ads. Keep all
four paper corners visible and tilt the page about 12 degrees.

## Architecture and nature collection

Create an exact 2×2 contact sheet with thick white gutters containing four distinct original
editorial photographs: a modern white concrete pavilion under blue sky, a quiet pine trail in morning
light, a geometric blue-and-white stair facade, and a calm mountain lake with a minimal cabin. No
people, text, logos, signs, famous landmarks, UI, watermark, or ads.
