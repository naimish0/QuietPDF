# Localization review

Upload-ready locale: **en-US only**.

The German, French, Japanese, and Arabic examples are layout tests generated from editable source,
not text painted over completed rasters. Every example is quarantined under
`localized/draft-not-for-upload/` and visibly stamped `DRAFT–NOT FOR UPLOAD`.

Required before any localized upload:

- Professional linguistic review in market.
- Product terminology review against localized application strings.
- Native punctuation and line-break review.
- Japanese glyph/font coverage review.
- Arabic font shaping, bidi, alignment, and RTL workflow review.
- Fresh no-ad and visual QA after regeneration.

Review result: **DRAFTS BLOCKED FROM UPLOAD**.
